◤ My Internship Journey ◢

During my internship, I had the opportunity to work in various capacities that allowed me to expand my skill set, contribute to projects, and gain real-world experience. Below is a summary of my role and key activities I was involved in.

Internship Location: Hospital Raja Permaisuri Bainun (HRPB), Ipoh, Perak
Role: Medical Records Intern
Duration: 8 weeks (July - September 2024)

Here are the main tasks I was involved in during my internship:

  • Handling and organizing patient medical records in the Medical Records Department.
  • Updating patient information in the Hospital Information System (HIS).
  • Ensuring the accuracy of patient information and medical reports.
  • Assisting in the registration of patient reports for post-mortem and ED under police control.

This internship gave me a solid understanding of healthcare operations and the critical importance of accurate medical records. I gained experience managing sensitive patient data, ensuring proper documentation, and maintaining confidentiality. I also learned how accurate medical records enhance patient care, streamline hospital operations, and support legal and insurance processes. This experience deepened my appreciation for the role of detailed record-keeping in delivering effective and efficient healthcare services.
